Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I order a custom cake?+
For standard custom cakes, we recommend at least 48 hours notice. For elaborate wedding or multi-tier cakes, please book 1–2 weeks in advance to ensure we can dedicate the artisan time your cake deserves.
Do you offer gluten-free or vegan options?+
Yes! We offer gluten-free sponge bases and fully vegan options using plant-based butter and aquafaba meringue. Please mention your dietary requirements in the special notes when ordering.
What areas do you deliver to?+
We currently deliver within Purwokerto, Banyumas, Purbalingga, and Cilacap. For locations outside these areas, please contact us directly — we may be able to arrange special delivery for larger orders.
Can I visit the bakery to taste test before ordering?+
Absolutely — we love meeting our clients! Tasting sessions are available Tuesday–Saturday from 10.00–17.00 by appointment. For wedding cake tastings, we recommend booking a dedicated 45-minute session via WhatsApp.
